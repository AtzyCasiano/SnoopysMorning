Park

Snoopy roams the park and searches for woodstock.He wonders where can he be. Then he remembers that woodstock favorite place is the benches. Snoopy walks toward the bench and he sees Woodstock sitting down eating a cookie. In the end Woodstock shares his cookies with snoopy and enjoy their morning in the park.
